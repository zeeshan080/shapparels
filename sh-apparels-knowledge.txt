Website: https://shapparels.vercel.app
Currency: Pakistani Rupee (PKR)
Country: Pakistan (nationwide delivery)
Payment Method: Cash on Delivery (COD) only


PRODUCT CATEGORIES

- Bags: Premium designer bags, handbags, tote bags, clutches, and crossbody bags for every occasion.
- Jewelry: Exquisite jewelry pieces including necklaces, earrings, bracelets, rings, and jewelry sets.

ORDERING PROCESS
----------------
1. Browse products on our website or ask our AI assistant for recommendations.
2. Add items to your cart.
3. Proceed to checkout and fill in your shipping details.
4. Confirm your order. Payment is Cash on Delivery only.
5. You will receive an order number (format: SH-YYYYMMDD-XXX) for tracking.
6. Your order will be delivered to your doorstep.

To place an order, customers need to provide:
- Full name
- Phone number
- Shipping address (street address, city)
- Province/state and zip code (optional)


SHIPPING POLICY
---------------
- We deliver across all of Pakistan.
- Estimated delivery time: 3-5 business days nationwide.
- Free shipping on orders of PKR 3,000 and above.
- A flat shipping fee of PKR 300 applies to orders below PKR 3,000.
- Orders are processed within 24 hours of confirmation.
- Customers receive tracking updates via WhatsApp.
- We currently do not offer international shipping.


RETURN & EXCHANGE POLICY
------------------------
- Returns are accepted within 7 days of delivery.
- Items can be returned for any reason within the 7-day window.
- To initiate a return, contact us via WhatsApp with your order number.
- Refunds will be processed within 3-5 business days after the returned item is received and inspected.
- Exchange is also available within the 7-day window, subject to stock availability.
- Shipping costs for returns are borne by the customer unless the item is defective or incorrect.
- Defective or wrong items will be replaced free of charge with free return shipping.
